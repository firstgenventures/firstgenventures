What we do

FGV connects top student-founders with like-minded peers and opportunities for investment.

Resources

Unlock potential and leap over hurdles with the support of a state-of-the-art work space, technical support, and access to special events and educational opportunities.

Investment

We invest up to $25,000 in companies when they first join the FGV family. At the pre-seed stage, FGV may additionally invest $25,000 - $100,000.

Commmunity

Portfolio companies receive mentorship from FGV advisors and student partners. Through our talent network, we help our partners recruit for both technical and non-technical roles.

Mentorship

We deliver value by connecting our companies to raise the capital they need through FGV's network. We host events with speakers from a wide variety of experiences in the tech, startup, and venture capital spaces.
